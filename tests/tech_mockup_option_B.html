NRR Trajectory

Deep research · 2F.2

NRR has compressed from 120%+ in FY22 to 107% in FY25 as enterprise SMB churn rose and seat growth slowed in mid-market. Cohort expansion in Data Cloud & Industries is offsetting mature Sales Cloud churn, but the ratio is structurally closer to mid-teens than peak. Trough hypothesis: 105% floor as AI-attach cohorts begin materially up-tiering in FY26.

AI Monetization Strategy

Deep research · 2F.7

Einstein Copilot priced at $30/user/month with Data Cloud as the consumption-based backplane. AI-attached ARR reached ~4% of total ARR in Q3, with mgmt targeting >10% by FY27. Agentforce launch in late 2025 is the call-option — per-action outcome pricing could re-rate the NRR ceiling if enterprise adoption mirrors early POCs.

M&A Capital Allocation

Deep research · 2F.8

Post-Slack integration learnings have shifted capital allocation toward buybacks over large deals; mgmt signaled tuck-ins only. Informatica dialogue paused at ~$30B price tag — discipline is a positive signal after activist pressure but caps the TAM-expansion growth narrative.
